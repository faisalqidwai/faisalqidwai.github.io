About Faisal Qidwai

Faisal Qidwai is a highly respected general practitioner with over 13 years of dedicated experience in medicine. Educated at the prestigious University of Sydney (MBBS, 2005) and a proud Fellow of the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners (FRACGP), he has built a reputation for providing innovative, evidence-based healthcare.

Known for his compassionate approach and patient-focused philosophy, Faisal emphasizes the importance of preventive care, early diagnosis, and holistic treatment plans tailored to the unique needs of each patient. His expertise spans chronic disease management, mental health support, and comprehensive wellness consultations. By staying abreast of the latest medical advancements, he ensures that his patients receive the most current and effective treatments available.

His commitment to excellence in healthcare is reflected in active community health education and fostering long-term patient relationships. Whether you are seeking routine health check-ups or specialized care, his personalized approach empowers patients to take control of their well-being.
